# Data Sources & Credits

## City of Toronto Open Data

- **Dataset:** Neighbourhoods
- **URL:** https://open.toronto.ca/dataset/neighbourhoods/
- **Licence:** [Open Government Licence – Toronto](https://open.toronto.ca/open-data-licence/)
- **Used for:** Neighbourhood boundary polygons (GeoJSON) displayed on the map

## Statistics Canada

- **Dataset:** Census 2021
- **URL:** https://www12.statcan.gc.ca/census-recensement/2021/
- **Licence:** [Statistics Canada Open Licence](https://www.statcan.gc.ca/en/reference/licence)
- **Used for:** Demographic data per neighbourhood (income, age, language, education)
